Mediapipe Holistic Web Demo

Drag and drop

Make "Three with thumb" with your right hand to turn on the interface.

Pull the thumb close or in front of the palm to grab and drag the object. To release the object, get back to the previous gesture.

Drag and drop

Palm picker

Make a "Palm" gesture with your right hand to open the picker. Rotate the palm to switch between items.

Make a "Four" gesture to confirm the picked item.

Palm picker

Zoom

 

Make a "Two with thumb" gesture with both hands to turn on the interface. Move both hands to transform the zoom square.

Zoom

Keyboard

Make "Three with thumb" with your left hand to turn on the interface.

Pull the thumb close or in front of the palm while hovering over the letter to add it to the text (left bottom). Get back to the previous gesture to select the next letter.

Keyboard

Touch

Make a "One" gesture with either left or right hand (or both at the same time) and hover fingertip near your lips/eye/ear for a split second to toggle microphone/camera/audio.

Touch
